Who we are

No hype. Just hemp, herbs, and heart.

Paws and Hemp was built out of frustration with the noise online: vague claims, biased reviews, and products that looked credible but had little behind them. The goal from the start was simple, honest guidance for pet owners trying to make good decisions, without having to wade through marketing spin to get there.

Every guide and product review on this site goes through the same filter: what does the evidence actually say, and does this genuinely help the animal? If the research is thin, that gets said. If a product does not meet the mark, it does not get recommended. No sponsored posts, no inflated ratings, no affiliate pressure overriding honest assessment.
